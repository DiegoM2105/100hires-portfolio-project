# YouTube Transcript Collection Method

## Purpose

This file documents how YouTube videos will be selected, collected, and organized for the AI-powered SEO content production research project.

The goal is not to collect a high volume of videos. The goal is to collect high-signal videos from selected experts that can support a future practical playbook.

## Selection Criteria

Videos will be selected based on the following criteria:

- The speaker is one of the selected experts or directly connected to their platform.
- The topic is relevant to AI-powered SEO content production, AI search, SEO strategy, content optimization, B2B SaaS content, or content distribution.
- The video includes practical insights, frameworks, examples, or strategic commentary.
- The material is recent when possible.
- The video can support a future playbook, not just provide general inspiration.

## Collection Process

For each relevant video, I will collect and document:

- Expert or channel name.
- Video title.
- Video URL.
- Publication date when available.
- Collection date.
- Transcript availability.
- Transcript collection method.
- Key themes.
- Practical takeaways.
- Potential playbook implications.

## Transcript Collection Methods

Depending on availability, transcripts may be collected through:

1. YouTube's built-in transcript feature.
2. YouTube caption data when available.
3. A free transcript extraction method or API when accessible.
4. Manual notes when transcripts are unavailable or incomplete.

If a full transcript is not available, I will document that limitation and collect structured notes instead.

## File Naming Convention

Transcript or transcript-note files will use a simple naming format:

expert-name-video-topic.md

Examples:

- aleyda-solis-ai-search-notes.md
- mike-king-ai-mode-notes.md
- ross-simmonds-content-distribution-notes.md
- romuald-fons-seo-ai-notes.md
- dean-romero-seo-content-notes.md

## Research Use

The collected transcript materials will be used to identify:

- Repeated expert themes.
- Practical workflows.
- AI search implications.
- SEO content production patterns.
- Distribution and repurposing ideas.
- Risks and quality concerns.
- Playbook-ready recommendations.

## Quality Standard

A video will only be included if it provides useful material for the research project.

This project prioritizes quality over volume. A small number of strong transcript notes is more valuable than many generic videos.
