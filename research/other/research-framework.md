# Research Framework: AI-Powered SEO Content Production

## Purpose

This framework defines what I am looking for while collecting expert content for the AI-powered SEO content production research project.

The goal is not to collect a large amount of generic content. The goal is to identify practical patterns, workflows, and strategic principles that could later support a real playbook for B2B SaaS teams.

## Core Research Questions

1. How are SEO and content experts adapting to AI search, AI Overviews, and generative search experiences?

2. How can AI support content research, briefing, optimization, and production without lowering editorial quality?

3. What role should human judgment play in AI-assisted SEO content workflows?

4. How can B2B SaaS companies use AI to scale content while still maintaining authority, differentiation, and relevance?

5. How should content teams think about distribution, repurposing, and visibility beyond traditional Google rankings?

## Signals to Look For

While reviewing expert content, I will look for the following signals:

- Practical workflows, not just opinions.
- Examples of AI being used to improve research, briefs, optimization, or distribution.
- Guidance on maintaining quality, originality, and trust.
- Mentions of AI search, AI Overviews, generative engine optimization, or answer engines.
- B2B SaaS relevance.
- Content operations or editorial process insights.
- Distribution and repurposing strategies.
- Risks or limitations of AI-generated content.
- Advice that could become part of a future tactical playbook.

## Expected Output

The collected material should be strong enough to support a future playbook on AI-powered SEO content production for B2B SaaS.

Potential playbook sections could include:

- How to choose SEO topics in an AI search environment.
- How to create AI-assisted content briefs.
- How to balance automation with editorial judgment.
- How to optimize content for Google, AI Overviews, and answer engines.
- How to repurpose SEO content across LinkedIn, newsletters, and YouTube.
- How to evaluate content quality before publishing.
- How to build a repeatable AI-assisted content workflow.
