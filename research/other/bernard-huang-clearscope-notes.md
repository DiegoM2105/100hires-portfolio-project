## Useful Research Angles

### 1. Content Optimization Workflows

Clearscope helps show how content teams can move from keyword research to structured content optimization.

For a future playbook, this suggests that AI-powered content workflows should include optimization checkpoints before publication.

### 2. Topic Coverage

AI-assisted SEO content should not only mention a keyword. It should cover the topic deeply enough to satisfy user intent and provide useful context.

Tools like Clearscope help identify related terms, questions, and subtopics that should be considered during briefing and editing.

### 3. AI Drafting and Human Editing

Clearscope includes AI drafting capabilities, but strong content still requires human editing.

This supports the research project's core idea: AI can accelerate content production, but human judgment is needed to validate accuracy, tone, positioning, and usefulness.

### 4. Topic Exploration and Query Expansion

Topic exploration is useful because AI search and modern SEO require broader topical understanding.

For B2B SaaS teams, this can help identify related buyer questions, pain points, comparison angles, and supporting subtopics.

### 5. AI Visibility Tracking

As search shifts toward AI-generated answers, tracking visibility in tools like ChatGPT, Google AI Overviews, and Perplexity becomes increasingly important.

This suggests that future SEO workflows may need to measure both traditional rankings and AI answer visibility.
