# Research Sources: AI-Powered SEO Content Production

## Research Topic

AI-powered SEO content production for B2B SaaS.

## Selection Criteria

The experts below were selected because they are active practitioners in SEO, content strategy, organic growth, AI search, generative engine optimization, or AI-assisted content workflows.

The goal is to prioritize high-signal voices who publish practical insights and have direct experience building, advising, or operating content and SEO systems.

This list intentionally combines international experts with Spanish-speaking practitioners. The international experts provide strong perspectives on AI search, B2B SaaS SEO, content operations, and generative engine optimization. The Spanish-speaking experts add regional depth and practical insight into how SEO, AI-assisted content, and digital publishing are being applied in Spanish-language markets.

Access date for initial source selection: June 19, 2026.
